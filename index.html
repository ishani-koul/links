SEIJAKU

(n) Stillness in motion

The Japanese speak of Seijaku as serenity in the midst of activity. Not escaping from the world to some mountaintop. But finding the real meaning, fulfillment, energy, and wisdom in the midst of everyday hustle and bustle — building a silent and imperturbable center while active in the disappointments and triumphs of our busy lives. This is real fulfillment.
